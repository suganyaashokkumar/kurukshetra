Guest lectures are an integral part of any technical festival. Every year, Kurukshetra features thought-provoking lectures by an array of extraordinary personalities, known for their eminence and eloquence. The stalwarts share their invaluable experiences, providing the audience with an excellent exposure to the emerging trends in technology. Interactive sessions and live demonstrations add to the action on stage.
